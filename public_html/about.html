Welcome to College Trade: Buy used goods from other students on your campus!

Founded in 2016, CollegeTrader was founded to help students buy and sell used goods from eachother. College trader allows students to get the items they need for affordable prices.

Too often students will buy new items to furnish their college living spaces. These college living spaces are merely temporary and students will often get rid of perfectly good furniture and other goods at the end of the semester. College trade seeks to connect students who are getting rid of necessary items with students who are about to purchase similar items.
